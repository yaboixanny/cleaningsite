Why Regular Gutter Cleaning is Essential

foundation

Prevent Foundation Damage

Clogged gutters overflow and dump water directly against your foundation, causing cracks, settling, and basement flooding. Proper drainage directs water away from your home, protecting your foundation and preventing costly structural repairs that can exceed $10,000.

roofing

Avoid Roof Damage

When gutters overflow, water backs up under roof shingles causing rot, leaks, and premature deterioration. In winter, ice dams form when clogged gutters prevent proper drainage, potentially damaging your roof structure and creating dangerous icicles.

pest_control

Eliminate Pest Habitats

Clogged gutters filled with decomposing leaves create perfect breeding grounds for mosquitoes, attract rodents, and provide nesting sites for birds and wasps. Clean gutters eliminate these pest habitats, keeping unwanted visitors away from your home.

spa

Protect Landscaping & Siding

Overflowing gutters erode flower beds, wash away mulch, and create unsightly trenches in your lawn. Water pouring down your siding causes staining, rot, and paint damage. Regular cleaning protects your landscaping investment and maintains your home's exterior beauty.

Our Simple 4-Step Process

1

Initial Inspection

We walk around your home to assess gutter condition, identify problem areas, and note any visible damage. We'll discuss your gutter system and provide a quote based on your home's size and gutter length.

2

Debris Removal & Flushing

Using safe ladder practices, we hand-scoop all leaves, twigs, and debris from your gutters. We then flush downspouts with water to clear any blockages and test that water flows properly away from your foundation.

3

Exterior Cleaning & Ground Cleanup

We clean the exterior of your gutters to remove streaks and buildup. All debris is bagged and removed from your property, leaving your yard clean. We also clear any debris from around your foundation and downspout areas.

4

Final Inspection & Report

We perform a final walkthrough to ensure all gutters are completely clear and draining properly. If we noticed any damage, loose hangers, or issues during cleaning, we'll inform you and provide recommendations for repairs.

Common Questions About Gutter Cleaning

How often should gutters be cleaned in Hamilton?

We recommend gutter cleaning at least twice per year for most Hamilton homes—once in late spring (May/June) and again in late fall (October/November). However, if you have many trees near your home, especially pine or oak trees, you may need quarterly cleanings. Heavy storms with wind and debris also necessitate inspections. Regular cleaning prevents clogs that can cause expensive water damage to your foundation, roof, and siding.

What happens if I don't clean my gutters regularly?

Neglected gutters lead to serious problems. Water overflows and pools around your foundation, causing cracks, basement flooding, and structural damage. Backed-up water damages roof shingles and fascia boards. In winter, ice dams form creating dangerous icicles and potential roof collapse. Clogged gutters also become heavy, pulling away from your house and requiring expensive repairs. Regular cleaning costs far less than fixing water damage.

How much does gutter cleaning cost in Hamilton?

Gutter cleaning in Hamilton typically costs between $100-$300 depending on your home's size, gutter length, height, and debris amount. Single-story homes with minimal debris are on the lower end, while two-story homes with heavily clogged gutters cost more. We offer discounted seasonal packages for twice-yearly service, which typically saves 15-20% compared to individual cleanings. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote.

Do you clean gutters in the winter?

We can clean gutters during mild winter days when temperatures are above freezing and conditions are safe. However, we recommend having gutters cleaned before winter arrives, typically in October or early November. Pre-winter cleaning prevents ice dams and ensures proper drainage during spring thaw. If you experience winter clogging or ice dam issues, contact us to assess whether safe cleaning is possible or if you need ice dam removal services.

Can you clean gutters on a two-story or three-story house?

Yes! We have the proper equipment and safety training to clean gutters on multi-story homes safely. We use extension ladders with stabilizers, follow strict safety protocols, and our technicians are experienced in working at heights. Whether you have a bungalow, two-story home, or three-story property, we can safely access and thoroughly clean your entire gutter system. We're fully insured for your protection.
