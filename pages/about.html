About Crystal Clear Window Washing

Family-owned and Hamilton-proud. Delivering exceptional window cleaning services since 2009.

Our Story

Crystal Clear Window Washing was founded in 2009 by a Hamilton family with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable window cleaning services that homeowners and business owners could trust. What started as a one-person operation with a squeegee and a ladder has grown into one of Hamilton's most trusted window cleaning companies.

Over the past 15+ years, we've cleaned thousands of homes and businesses throughout Hamilton and surrounding communities. But despite our growth, we've never forgotten our roots. We're still a family-owned business, and we still treat every customer like a neighbor—because in Hamilton, they are.

Our reputation has been built one satisfied customer at a time. We've grown through word-of-mouth referrals, positive reviews, and repeat business from clients who appreciate our attention to detail, professionalism, and commitment to quality. We take pride in every job we complete, whether it's a small residential home or a large commercial building.
