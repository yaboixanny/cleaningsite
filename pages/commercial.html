Common Questions About Commercial Window Cleaning

How often should commercial windows be cleaned?

For most businesses, we recommend professional window cleaning every 1-3 months. High-traffic retail locations and restaurants may benefit from monthly or bi-weekly service, while office buildings typically need quarterly cleaning. The frequency depends on your location, foot traffic, environmental factors, and industry standards. We'll help you determine the optimal schedule for your business.

Can you clean windows after business hours?

Yes! We understand that window cleaning during business hours can be disruptive. We offer flexible scheduling including early morning, evening, and weekend service to minimize impact on your operations. Many of our commercial clients prefer after-hours cleaning, and we're happy to accommodate your schedule.

Do you have insurance and proper licensing for commercial work?

Absolutely. Crystal Clear Window Washing carries comprehensive commercial general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. All our technicians are bonded and background-checked. We're happy to provide proof of insurance and add your business as an additional insured on our policy if required by your property management or lease agreement.

What types of commercial properties do you service?

We service all types of commercial properties including office buildings, retail stores, restaurants, medical facilities, schools, churches, fitness centers, car dealerships, and more. Whether you have a small storefront or a multi-story building, we have the equipment and expertise to handle your window cleaning needs professionally and safely.

How much does commercial window cleaning cost in Hamilton?

Commercial window cleaning costs vary based on building size, number of windows, accessibility, and service frequency. Prices typically range from $100-$500+ per service for small to medium businesses. We offer discounted rates for recurring maintenance contracts. Contact us for a free, detailed quote based on your specific property and needs.
