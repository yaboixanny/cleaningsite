Common Questions About Residential Window Cleaning

How often should I have my home windows cleaned?

Most homeowners benefit from professional window cleaning 2-4 times per year. However, the ideal frequency depends on your location, surrounding trees, weather exposure, and personal preferences. Homes near busy roads or in wooded areas may need more frequent cleanings. We can help you create a custom schedule that keeps your windows looking their best year-round.

Do I need to be home during the window cleaning?

For interior window cleaning, yes, someone needs to be home to provide access. For exterior-only service, you don't need to be present as long as we have access to the property and any gates are unlocked. Many clients schedule exterior cleanings while they're at work. We'll coordinate with you to find the most convenient arrangement.

What if it rains after you clean my windows?

Light rain won't harm freshly cleaned windows! In fact, rainwater is relatively pure and won't leave spots. Dirt and spots come from dust, pollen, and pollutants that stick to windows when they're dry. If heavy weather causes issues within 48 hours of your service, just let us know and we'll return to touch up any affected areas at no charge.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Absolutely. We use eco-friendly, biodegradable cleaning solutions that are completely safe for your family and pets. Our products are non-toxic and won't harm plants, landscaping, or the environment. We never use harsh chemicals like ammonia that can cause fumes or leave residue.

Can you clean windows on a two-story or three-story home?

Yes! We have specialized equipment including extension poles and professional ladders to safely clean windows on multi-story homes. Our technicians are trained in proper ladder safety and use stabilizers to protect your siding and gutters. We can reach windows up to three stories high as part of our standard residential service.
