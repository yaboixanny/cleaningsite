Common Questions About High-Rise Window Cleaning

How do you clean windows on tall buildings safely?

We use multiple safety-certified methods depending on your building configuration: rope access (rappelling from the roof), bosun's chairs, swing stages, and water-fed pole systems. All our technicians are IRATA-certified with extensive fall protection training. We use redundant safety systems, conduct daily equipment inspections, and follow strict safety protocols approved by WorkSafeBC and OSHA standards.

How much does high-rise window cleaning cost in Hamilton?

High-rise window cleaning typically costs more than standard services due to specialized equipment and safety requirements. Prices generally range from $3-$8 per window depending on building height, accessibility, and total window count. Most buildings benefit from 2-4 cleanings per year. We offer significant discounts for annual maintenance contracts with scheduled cleanings throughout the year.

What is the maximum building height you can service?

Our team can service buildings of any height. For buildings up to 6 stories, we often use water-fed pole systems which are efficient and cost-effective. For taller buildings, we employ rope access techniques with IRATA-certified technicians who have experience working on buildings exceeding 20 stories. We assess each building individually to determine the safest and most efficient cleaning method.

Do you need access to our building's roof?

For rope access cleaning, yes, we need roof access with suitable anchor points. During our site inspection, we'll verify that your building has proper tie-off points or we can arrange for certified anchor installation. For water-fed pole systems (up to 6 stories), roof access is not required. We coordinate all access arrangements with building management during the planning phase.

How long does high-rise window cleaning take?

The duration varies based on building size, height, and number of windows. A typical 10-story building with 200 windows takes 1-2 days to complete. Larger buildings may require 3-5 days. We work efficiently to minimize the time while maintaining our quality standards. Weather conditions can affect scheduling, and we may need to reschedule if conditions become unsafe for high-altitude work.
