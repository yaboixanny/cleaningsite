Benefits of Professional Screen Service

air

Improved Airflow

Clean screens allow up to 30% more air to flow through your windows. Remove the buildup of dust, pollen, and debris that restricts fresh air circulation, creating a healthier and more comfortable indoor environment.

visibility

Better Visibility

Dirty screens obscure your view and reduce natural light. Professional cleaning restores clarity and brightness, letting you enjoy unobstructed views of the outdoors while still keeping insects out.

Screens trap pollen, dust mites, and other allergens that can trigger respiratory issues. Regular screen cleaning removes these irritants, creating a healthier living space especially for those with allergies or asthma.

Regular cleaning and timely repairs prevent minor damage from becoming major problems. Our maintenance services extend the life of your screens, saving you money on premature replacement costs.

Our Simple 4-Step Process

1

Screen Inspection

We carefully inspect each screen for damage, tears, bent frames, and overall condition. We document any issues and provide recommendations for cleaning, repair, or replacement based on your screens' needs.

2

Gentle Cleaning Process

We remove screens and use specialized brushes and cleaning solutions to gently scrub away dirt, pollen, and grime. Each screen is thoroughly rinsed and dried to prevent water spots and ensure complete cleanliness.

Repairs & Re-Screening

If repairs are needed, we fix small tears with patches, straighten bent frames, and replace damaged screening material. For severely damaged screens, we can provide complete re-screening or new screen installation.

We carefully reinstall each screen, ensuring proper fit and secure placement. We test screens for smooth operation and conduct a final quality inspection to ensure you're completely satisfied with the results.

Common Questions About Screen Services

How often should window screens be cleaned?

We recommend professional screen cleaning at least once per year, ideally in spring or early summer. Homes near busy roads, in high-pollen areas, or with many trees may benefit from twice-yearly cleaning. Regular cleaning prevents buildup that can damage the mesh and improves air quality in your home. Many homeowners schedule screen cleaning when they have their windows cleaned.

Can you repair torn or damaged window screens?

Yes! We can repair small tears and holes using professional patch kits that blend seamlessly with your existing mesh. For larger damage or severely worn screens, we offer complete re-screening services where we replace the mesh while keeping your existing frame. If frames are damaged beyond repair, we can also provide full screen replacement.

Do you remove screens to clean them or clean them in place?

For the most thorough cleaning, we remove screens and clean them individually using specialized brushes and cleaning solutions. This method allows us to clean both sides completely, inspect for damage, and ensure proper drying before reinstallation. Removing screens also allows us to clean the window tracks and frames underneath where dirt accumulates.

How much does screen cleaning cost in Hamilton?

Screen cleaning typically costs $3-$8 per screen depending on size and condition. When bundled with window cleaning, we offer discounted rates starting at $2-$5 per screen. Screen repairs range from $15-$30 per screen, while complete re-screening costs $25-$50 per screen depending on size. Contact us for an exact quote based on your specific needs.

What types of window screens can you service?

We service all types of window screens including standard fiberglass mesh, aluminum mesh, pet-resistant screens, solar screens, and specialty privacy screens. We handle sliding screens, fixed screens, and removable screens for both residential and commercial properties. If you have custom or unusual screens, we can assess them during our inspection and recommend the best cleaning or repair approach.
